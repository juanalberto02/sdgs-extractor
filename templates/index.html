What is SDGs Extractor for users?

The SDGs Extractor helps you quickly identify which SDGs your research supports. Upload a PDF and the system will automatically extract Title, Abstract, and Keywords, then match them against curated SDG rules to produce top-3 SDGs with similarity scores and clear evidence.

Auto-extract & detect

Explainable outputs
